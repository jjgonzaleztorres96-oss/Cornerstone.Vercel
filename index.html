Common questions.

How much does it cost to paint a room?
Room painting typically ranges from $300–$800 depending on size, ceiling height, and condition. We provide exact written quotes after a free on-site visit — no guesswork, no surprises at the end.
How long does a typical project take?
A single room takes 1–2 days. A full interior averages 3–5 days. Exterior projects typically run 3–7 days depending on size and prep work. We'll give you a clear timeline in your written estimate.
Do I need to move my furniture?
No. We move and protect all furniture as part of our prep process. Floors are covered, items are repositioned, and everything is put back when we're done. You just show up to a finished room.
What type of paint do you use?
We use Sherwin-Williams and Benjamin Moore premium lines as our standard. We'll recommend the right sheen and formula for each surface — and you're welcome to specify a brand or product if you have a preference.
Are you insured?
Yes. We carry full general liability insurance and will provide the certificate before any work begins. You should never let a painter work in your home without one.
Do you guarantee your work?
All our work comes with a 2-year workmanship warranty. If anything isn't right — peeling, cracking, missed spots — we come back and make it right at no charge.
What areas do you serve?
We serve the Greater Toronto Area including Richmond Hill, Vaughan, Thornhill, Woodbridge, King City, Markham, Aurora, and Newmarket. Not sure if we cover your area? Give us a call.
