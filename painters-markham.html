Common Questions
About Painting in Markham

Do you paint heritage homes in Unionville?

Yes. Heritage homes often have specific surface challenges — older plaster, wood panelling, or multi-layered paint. We know how to prepare and finish these surfaces properly.

Which Markham neighbourhoods do you cover?

All of Markham, including Unionville, Old Markham Village, Cornell, Cathedraltown, Milliken, Thornhill (Markham side), and Angus Glen.

What should I do to prepare my home for the painters?

We'll advise you during the estimate walkthrough. Generally: clear walls of pictures, move small items away from walls, and ensure our crew has access to all areas being painted.

Are you fully insured for work in Markham?

Yes — we carry full general liability insurance. You'll receive a copy of our insurance certificate before any work begins.
