ABOUT US

Painters you can actually trust.

We are a Toronto-based painting company with over 15 years of experience working in homes and businesses across the GTA. This is what we do every day, and we take it seriously.

OUR STORY

At Cornerstone Painting, we believe your home or business deserves to feel cared for, respected, and beautifully finished. With over 15 years of experience, our team has worked on everything from single rooms to full commercial buildings across Toronto and the GTA.

Over the years we have learned that great painting is not only about applying paint. It is about preparation, trust, cleanliness, and treating every space like it matters. Cornerstone was built on honest communication, quality workmanship, and genuine respect for the people who invite us into their homes and businesses.

We know that letting a crew into your space is a real act of trust. So we focus on making the whole process smooth and stress-free, from the first conversation to the final walkthrough. Our goal is simple: leave you with more than a fresh coat of paint. We want you to feel proud of your space.
