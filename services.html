MORE SERVICES

Specialty Services

Beyond painting, we handle the prep and finishing work that most painters subcontract or skip. One crew, one invoice, no coordination headaches.

Drywall Repairs

Holes, cracks, water stains, and damaged corners — patched, sanded, and primed before we paint. The repair becomes invisible. We handle everything from small nail pops to large section replacements.

Popcorn Ceiling Removal

Textured ceilings scraped clean, skimmed smooth, and painted — leaving a flat, modern finish. We contain the mess completely. Most rooms are done in a single day with minimal disruption.

Taping & Painting

New drywall installations need taping, mudding, and sanding before paint can go on. We do the full sequence in-house — no waiting on a separate taper. Seams disappear completely under the final coat.

Staining

Decks, fences, hardwood floors, and interior wood features — properly sanded, cleaned, and stained to bring out the natural grain. Solid stains, semi-transparent, and clear-coat finishes all available.
